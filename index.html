About Us

Who we are

Founded in 2023 Revco Trading Co.W.L.L, headquartered in Riffa-Bharin with a network of locations in almost all areas of Kingdom providing services in Oil & Gas, Rigs, General Trading, General Contracting, Pipeline, , Equipment Rental, Transportation and Logistic Service to a broad range of commercial and industrial clients throughout the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, including many fortune Companies.
Anchored by loyal employees who are dedicated to providing superior service, and grounded in the philosophy that honesty and integrity are as important as the bottom line, Revco Trading Co.W.L.L has enjoyed an impeccable reputation for an year.

VISION

Our vision is to become a global brand the world can trust. Our dedicated team simply try harder, and the results speak for themselves.

MISSION

Revco Trading Co.W.L.L mission is to ensure the best quality services and product on time at an a affordable cost without any hidden tricks.

Services

  • RIG SERVICES

    The Rig Service division at HM Al Harbi provides technical and logistical support to both offshore and onshore rigs within the kingdom. Our team comprises certified technicians with the expertise to operate in diverse terrains. Whether on land or at sea, our skilled professionals ensure efficient and reliable assistance, contributing to the seamless functioning of rigs. At HM Al Harbi, we prioritize excellence in technical support, facilitating the optimal performance of both offshore and onshore operations in the Kingdom.

  • OIL & GAS SERVICES

    HM Al Harbi's Oil & Gas division pioneers innovative technologies to address the escalating energy demands globally. Established in 2023, Revco Trading Co.W.L.L has been a trailblazer in delivering support services and comprehensive information on the Saudi Oil and Gas Market. Our success is intricately linked to a dedicated team of professionals guided by a visionary approach, acknowledging that energy lies at the core of our endeavors. Throughout our operations, we strive to foster mutual understanding and collaboration with esteemed companies worldwide, underlining our commitment to advancing the energy sector and meeting evolving needs.

  • INDUSTRIAL SERVICES

    HM Al Harbi Industrial Service boasts a versatile technical team proficient in handling diverse project categories, whether within plant facilities or in outdoor settings. Our accomplished track record is a testament to our team's capability, showcasing successful project completions over time. We are equipped to execute a wide range of projects with precision and efficiency, emphasizing our commitment to delivering exceptional industrial services.

  • GENERAL TRADING

    Drawing upon our expertise and strategic Middle East location, HM Al Harbi General Trading taps into significant market potential within the region. With a focus on delivering high-quality products at competitive prices, we maintain a diverse customer base. Our extensive business knowledge enables us to supply reputable companies in the Oil and Gas, drilling, and project support sectors. Over time, HM Al Harbi has upheld a distinguished position in the trading business, consistently updating our customers with timely introductions of new products and services, contributing to our sustained reputation and success in the industry.

  • GENERAL CONTRACTING

    HM Al Harbi Contracting is fueled by top-quality construction materials procured from trusted vendors. Our expertise spans Industrial Civil Construction, Mechanical, Electrical & Instrumentation, Telecommunications, Electrical Construction, General Maintenance, Temporary Building Facilities, Manpower, and Equipment Rental Services. We provide a diverse range of highly skilled individuals with robust technical experience, contributing to various facets of construction and related services. With a commitment to excellence, HM Al Harbi delivers comprehensive solutions across the construction spectrum, ensuring reliability and proficiency in every aspect of our offerings.

  • MANPOWER SUPPLY & TECHNICAL SUPPORT

    HM Al Harbi's Manpower Technical Support division stands as a premier consultancy, dedicated to providing highly skilled personnel to esteemed companies such as Saudi Aramco, SABIC, SECO, etc. since 2022. Leveraging our extensive experience in Project Support services, we excel in recruiting proficient and trustworthy workforce globally. This approach not only saves time and energy but also minimizes expenses and complications for our clients, reinforcing our commitment to delivering efficient and cost-effective solutions in the realm of manpower support.
